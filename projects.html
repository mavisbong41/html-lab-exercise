Project: Etiqa+ eMoney Insurance Portal

This project is a mobile insurance management system that simplifies the process of purchasing and renewing policies through eMoney. I was responsible for designing the user interface for real-time payment calculations and fraud detection. It aims to provide a seamless experience for policyholders to manage their insurance in one centralized platform with new payment feature of eMoney.
